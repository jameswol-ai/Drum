## 🛠️ Engineering Lab

DRUM now includes a **real structural engineering analysis** tool.

- **Load Calculation** – dead + live loads for the whole floor.
- **Span & Beam Selection** – automatic suggestion of steel profiles (IPE series).
- **Safety Factor** – simple integrity check.
- **Cost Estimation** – concrete, steel, glass, labour.
- **Energy Efficiency Score** – based on orientation and glazing ratio.

All analyses are performed on the currently active building (generated in the Evolution Chamber). Results can be logged and saved to your user profile.
